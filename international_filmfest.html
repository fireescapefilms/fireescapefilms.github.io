Diaspora Film Festival 2026

We are currently accepting submissions!

We invite young filmmakers to showcase their stories at the Diaspora International Film Festival, to be hosted at International House at the University of Chicago on May 8th, 2026. This festival will celebrate the creativity and voices of student artists exploring themes of identity, migration, and cultural heritage.

The film festival is hosted by Fire Escape Films and the International House at the University of Chicago.

Eligibility:

  • Open to filmmakers worldwide under the age of 30.
  • Films must align with the festival theme, "Latin American Diaspora." The creator (director/writer/producer) is of Latin American descent and/or the theme of the film touches upon immigration/diaspora/cross- culture/Latin American culture/etc.
  • Preference will be given to filmmakers who can attend the event in person (travel will not be funded).

Submission Guidelines:

  • Films must be under 20 minutes in length.
  • A working link to an online version of your film on FilmFreeway and/or Vimeo and any necessary passwords to view the film MUST be uploaded at the time of submission, along with your entry fee. Please note: This link must remain active and working through May 2026.
  • Each entry must be accompanied by its own entry form and fee.
  • The resolution should be Full HD or 4K.
With the submission of this film I agree to the following:
  • I am authorized to submit this film/project to the DFF for consideration.
  • I have obtained all necessary rights and clearances.
  • While every precaution will be taken to safeguard all materials submitted, I release the DFF from all responsibility for loss of or damage to submitted materials.
  • I have read, understand and agree to all the requirements as stated in the above "General Festival Rules."

Selection Criteria:

The films will be judged by a committee of UChicago Faculty, Graduate Students in the Cinema and Media Studies department, and non-UChicago affiliated reviewers. The films will be evaluated based on:

  • Artistic Expression.
  • Adherence to theme.
  • Technical Execution.
Diaspora Film Festival proudly accepts entries on FilmFreeway, the world's #1 way to enter film festivals and creative contests.

Please contact fecommittee@gmail.com for any questions and problems you meet when submitting to the festival.
