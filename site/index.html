Make a movie this quarter with Fire Escape Films!

We have two production cycles each year: the fall quarter production, and the winter-spring quarter production. We work as crews to produce short films in 8 weeks. There is a variety of roles for you just like in the industry!

How it works

Your journey to a film production in 3 simple steps

01

Subscribe to our email newsletter!

We use emails to send you the latest updates on our production cyles, and it is essential for you to be able to join a crew in time. We are still developing this website to ensure you can subscribe from the form below.

02

Come to our first general meeting!

At the beginning of each quarter, we will host a general meeting for directors to share their production needs and for you to sign up as a crew member. The first meeting is VERY IMPORTANT for anyone who wants to participate in one of Fire Escape Films' productions.

03

Start working with your crew!

After talking to the director during the first general meeting, you should have exchanged contact information with your crew members. And yes, you can start working on your film production!
