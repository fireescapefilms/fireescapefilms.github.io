    Fire Escape Films - Filmmaking Information    

Proposing a New Film

   

Please contact fecommittee@gmail.com for questions.    


Fire Escape Films has two regular production cycles:

    1. the Fall Production Cycle, and
    2. the Winter - Spring Production Cycle.

We put up open calls for each cycle about a month before they begin. These are communicated through our Instagram, and through our list-host (sign up HERE). Extemporaneous applications are unusual, yet still considered on a case by case basis, only for Indie Film Projects. If you wish to propose one, contact us through email.

We support three types of film projects:

    1. Indie Projects: Short films that don't require a ton of funding or crew. Smaller scale projects that are given more flexibility. Up to 10 minutes.
    2. Regular Projects: Short films that are regularly supported by Fire Escape Films. These are the ones that we have supported for the longest, and the ones that have all of the classic FE resources and support. Up to 20 minutes.
    3. Extended Projects: Short films (that may not be so short). Only for seasoned FE members, who are looking to do a more ambitious project. Up to 25 minutes.

All of these projects will have a Development Manager assigned, and are eligible to use Fire Escape Equipment.
